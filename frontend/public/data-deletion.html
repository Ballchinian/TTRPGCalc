Data Deletion

TTRPG Calculator · Last updated: 23 June 2026

You can remove the data TTRPG Calculator holds about you in the following ways.

Delete individual content

While signed in, you can delete any character, weapon, spell, or saved battle you created directly from within the app. Deleting a character also removes its uploaded portrait image.

Delete your entire account

To permanently delete your account and all data associated with it — your name and email, login credentials, characters, actions, saved battles, and uploaded images — email a deletion request to:

[ your contact email ]

Please send the request from the email address on your account, or include the name/email you signed up with (or the account you used to sign in with Google or Facebook). We will permanently delete your data within 30 days and confirm by email once it is done.
