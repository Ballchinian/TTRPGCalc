Privacy Policy

TTRPG Calculator · Last updated: 23 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information TTRPG Calculator ("the app", "we") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By creating an account or signing in you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account details: your name and email address. If you register with a password, we store only a securely hashed version of it (never the plain password). If you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your name, email address, and that provider's account identifier.
  • Content you create: characters, weapons, spells, saved battles, and any character portrait images you upload.
  • Session cookie: a single secure, httpOnly cookie used to keep you signed in. It is essential for the app to function and is not used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

We do not run third-party advertising or analytics trackers, and we do not sell your data.

How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and keep you signed in.
  • To store and display the characters, actions, and battles you create.
  • To send transactional emails you request, such as password-reset links.

Sharing and third-party services

We share data only with the service providers needed to run the app:

  • Google and Facebook — only if you choose to sign in with them, to verify your identity.
  • Cloudinary — hosts character portrait images you upload.
  • Our database and hosting providers — store the app's data and serve the site.
  • Our email provider — delivers password-reset emails.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. You can delete individual characters, actions, and battles from within the app at any time. To delete your entire account and all associated data, see our Data Deletion page.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time using the contact details below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
